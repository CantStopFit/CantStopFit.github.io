Forearm or Straight Arm Plank

2 Minute Plank Total Time

How To Complete This Challenge

Get down like you are about to do a push up and then you can bend your arms to rest on your forearms or you can keep them straight. Ensure that you back is flat! Hold this for as long as you can, stop the timer when you fall and start it when you get back up again. Try not to spend to much time on the floor.

Super Challenge

Hold the plank for as long as you can even after time expires
