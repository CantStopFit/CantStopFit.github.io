Push-Ups

25 Reps

How To Complete This Challenge

First, get into a plank position and put your hands shoulder width apart and your legs spread about the same width as your hands. Then, keeping a straight back, go down and then back up while staying on your toes. DO NOT touch the floor with your belly, your goal is to get as close to the floor as you can without touching. If you need to modify, get on your knees and push up from that position keeping the same hand and leg position.

Super Challenge

Do as many as you can in a row without stopping or double the ammount of pushups you do!
