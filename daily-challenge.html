Daily Challenge

Want a Challenge? Heres one...

Situps

2 Sets of 25 Reps

How To Complete This Challenge

Laydown on the ground with your heels close to your but so that your legs create a pyramid or mountain shape. Use your upperbody strength to bring your chest to your knees. Cross your arms on your chest to or interlace your fingers behind your head. Do NOT use your arms to propell you up!

Super Challenge

Instead of 2 sets of 25, try 3 sets of 25!
